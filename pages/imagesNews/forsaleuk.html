SECOND HAND X-302 / FOR SALE

Cliquez ici pour la version française.

Xtra-Time is the X-302 mk2 hull#40. The boat was built in June 1988. The boat specifications are available here. The engine has been professionally maintained every year . Results of the boats are available on this page and includes 2nd at Cowes Week 2004, 2nd at Myth of Malham 2005, 1st at Spi Ouest-France 2007...

 

Hull


The boat has been dryed stored during 3 month every year. Antifouling is Nautix A4T Speed, and was sprayed every year. The epoxy layer has been redone in 2002 and 2006. In 2006, the boat was stored in a closed yard to get the right temperature during the epoxy spray.


Cruising options

Sprayhood cockpit. 2 lockers in fore cabin. 2 additionals lockers in the saloon. New furling genoa (never installed).


Rigging

Custom carbon mast designed by Nordic Mast in 2004. Rod rigging. Spectra backstay. Harken carbofoil forestay.


Instruments

ST60 Speed
ST60 Wind
ST40 Depth
ST40 Compas
VHF et GPS .
Auto Pilot ST2000+


Engine

Yanmar 17HP : saildrive.


Sails

All the sails have been designed by ALR Saildesign (check his result page)

4 Mains (1 cruising, 1 pentex 2 "Offshore", 1 Kevlar 2004, 1 Carbon GPL 2007)

8 Genoas ( 4 kevlar ligh(2007,2005,2003,2002 (mylard)) 4 kevlar medium(2006,2004,2002,1998 (mylard)))

4 Spinnakers (light 2007, heavy 2005, small heavy 2003, small light 2002)

2 solents (Kevlar 2005, Dacron 2003) + un cruising OC


Price : 82 000 Euros or 74 000 Euros with an aluminium mast and whithout the 2007 sails. Price includes VAT.
